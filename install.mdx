---
title: "Installing & uninstalling"
description: "How Porttera apps add themselves to your theme — and how to remove them cleanly."
---

Every Porttera app is a **Shopify theme app extension**. That means it installs as a block
you place in the theme editor, not as code pasted into your `theme.liquid`.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install from the App Store">
    Add the app from the Shopify App Store. It requests only the access it needs to run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the theme editor">
    Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the block">
    On the relevant template (product, cart, etc.), click **Add block** and choose the
    Porttera app block. Drag it where you want it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save the theme. The block is live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing an app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Remove the block">In the theme editor, delete the app's block from your templates.</Step>
  <Step title="Uninstall">Uninstall the app from **Settings → Apps and sales channels**.</Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Because nothing is injected into your theme, uninstalling leaves no leftover code behind.
</Check>

<Tip>
  Works on any **Online Store 2.0** theme. Switching themes? Re-add the block on the new
  theme — your settings stay with the app.
</Tip>
