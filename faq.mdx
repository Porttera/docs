---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Common questions about the Porttera apps."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do the apps slow my store down?" icon="gauge-high">
    No. Every app installs as a theme app extension with lightweight, deferred scripts — and
    nothing is injected into your theme's layout. They're built to stay out of the way of your
    page speed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do they inject code into my theme?" icon="code">
    No. Each app is a block you add in the theme editor. There's no pasted snippet in
    `theme.liquid`, and uninstalling leaves nothing behind.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I uninstall?" icon="trash">
    The block stops rendering and the data the app held is removed (except records we're
    legally required to keep, like billing). Your theme is untouched.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which themes are supported?" icon="palette">
    Any **Online Store 2.0** theme. If you switch themes, just re-add the block on the new one —
    your settings stay with the app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do the apps work together?" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Yes. Wholesale, Product Customizer, and Bundles are designed to run side by side on the
    same store without conflicts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is billing handled?" icon="credit-card">
    Through Shopify, under the plan shown when you install. Cancel any time by uninstalling.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data safe?" icon="shield-halved">
    We process only the data needed to run the app you've installed, and we don't sell it or
    use it for advertising. See the [privacy policy](https://porttera.com/pages/privacy).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get support?" icon="life-ring">
    Use the [contact form](https://porttera.com/pages/contact) — it reaches the person who
    built the apps, not a ticket queue.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
