Configure Wholesale from the **block settings** in the theme editor and from the **app admin**.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Partner tag" icon="tag">
    The customer tag that unlocks wholesale pricing. Default: `partner`. You can use any tag
    you like.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Price source" icon="dollar-sign">
    Where the wholesale price comes from — a variant **metafield**, a **percentage off** the
    retail price, or a fixed **price list**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Negotiation floor" icon="shield-halved">
    The lowest price the AI assistant may offer. Quotes never go below it. Leave negotiation
    off to disable quotes entirely.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Display" icon="eye">
    Show wholesale pricing on product pages, collection grids, or both.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Start with **percentage off** to launch fast, then move to metafield pricing when you need
  per-variant control.
</Tip>
