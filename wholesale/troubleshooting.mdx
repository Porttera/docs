---
title: "Troubleshooting"
description: "Common Wholesale issues and how to fix them."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A partner doesn't see wholesale pricing" icon="user-xmark">
    Check, in order:
    - The customer has the exact **partner tag** (default `partner` — tags are case-sensitive and must match your setting).
    - They're **logged in** to their account on the storefront.
    - The **Wholesale price block** is added to the product template in the theme editor.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The wrong price shows" icon="dollar-sign">
    The price comes from your **Price source** setting:
    - **Metafield** — confirm the `wholesale.price` variant metafield is set for that variant.
    - **Percentage off** — confirm the percentage is correct.
    - Clear your browser cache or test in a private window to rule out a cached page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The wholesale price isn't charged at checkout" icon="cart-shopping">
    Make sure **checkout pricing** is enabled in the app admin. If you're using discount codes
    elsewhere, confirm they aren't conflicting — Wholesale doesn't need codes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Negotiation / quotes aren't working" icon="comments">
    - Confirm **negotiation is turned on** in settings.
    - Confirm a **price floor** is set (the assistant won't run without one).
    - Quotes create a **draft order** for your approval — check **Orders → Drafts**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Price shows on the product but not on collection grids (or vice versa)" icon="grip">
    Adjust the **Display** setting to show wholesale pricing on product pages, collection
    grids, or both.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
