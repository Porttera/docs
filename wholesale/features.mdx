## Inline pricing
Replaces your theme's price with the partner price, **in place** — on product pages and
collection grids, including infinite scroll. No flash, no separate line.

## AI negotiation
Partners request a quote; the assistant replies with an offer **above your floor**. Accepted
quotes create a **draft order** for your approval before anything is charged.

## Charged at checkout
The wholesale price is what's charged automatically — no manual discount codes to manage.

## Quick order grid
A fast grid so partners can order many variants at once.

## Partner-only
Only customers tagged `partner` ever see wholesale pricing. Everyone else sees retail.

## No code
Installs as a clean theme block and works on any Online Store 2.0 theme.
