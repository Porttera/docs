## Live designer
Shoppers pick options, add text and artwork, and preview live on the **real product image** —
not a generic mockup.

## Per-side personalization
Front, back, and sides — each with its own decoration, text, and color.

## Artwork on the order
Uploaded art is stored in your **Shopify Files** and attached to the order for fulfilment.

## Print-ready files
Download customer artwork at full resolution for production.

## Saved designs
Customers save designs to their account, reopen them, and **reorder** the exact same thing
in a click.

## Lightweight
Fast, deferred scripts and no theme code — review-ready out of the box.
