---
title: "Settings"
description: "Configure the designer per product."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Decoration areas" icon="layer-group">
    Define the sides shoppers can decorate (front, back, left, right) and the printable bounds
    for each.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Inputs" icon="font">
    Choose which inputs are available — custom **text**, **color** swatches, and **image
    upload** — per area.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Uploads" icon="image">
    Allowed file types and a maximum file size for customer artwork.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Saved designs" icon="floppy-disk">
    Let signed-in customers save and reorder designs from their account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Keep the printable bounds tight to your real print area so previews match what ships.
</Tip>
