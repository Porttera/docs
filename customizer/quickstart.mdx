---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Put the designer on a product in minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Add Product Customizer from the Shopify App Store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the designer block">
    In the theme editor, open a **product** template and add the **Customizer** block.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define options">
    In the app admin, set the decoration areas (front, back, sides) and the inputs shoppers
    can use — text, colors, and image upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Save and publish. Shoppers can now design that product.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Open the product, add some text and an image, and place a test order — the artwork and
  selections should appear on the order in your admin.
</Check>
