<Update label="v1.0.0" description="Launch">
  The first public release — three apps, all installing as clean theme app extensions.

  **Wholesale**
  - Inline partner pricing on product pages and collection grids
  - AI-assisted negotiation with a price floor you control
  - Wholesale price charged automatically at checkout

  **Product Customizer**
  - Live product designer with preview on the real product image
  - Saved designs customers can reopen and reorder
  - Artwork stored in Shopify Files and attached to every order

  **Bundles**
  - Fixed or percentage-off bundle pricing
  - Savings shown on the page, one-click add-to-cart
  - Inventory and discounts kept in sync
</Update>
