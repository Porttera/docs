---
title: "Troubleshooting"
description: "Common Bundles issues and how to fix them."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The bundle doesn't show on the page" icon="eye-slash">
    - Confirm the **Bundle block** is added to the product (or bundle page) in the theme editor.
    - Confirm the bundle is **published** in the app admin.
    - Republish the theme after adding the block.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The savings aren't displayed" icon="badge-percent">
    Turn on the **savings badge** in settings. With **fixed** pricing, the savings are shown
    against the combined item total.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Only one item is added to the cart" icon="cart-shopping">
    Re-check the bundle's **item list** in the app admin — every component must be listed. If an
    item is unavailable, it won't be added (see stock, below).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A bundle is unavailable or partially adds" icon="boxes-stacked">
    Bundles respect each item's **stock**. If a component is out of stock, the bundle can't be
    completed — restock the item or swap it out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The price looks wrong" icon="dollar-sign">
    Check the **pricing type**: **fixed** sets one total price; **percentage off** discounts the
    combined total. Confirm the value, then test in a private window to avoid a cached page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Still stuck? [Contact us](https://porttera.com/pages/contact) with the bundle and your store URL.
</Note>
