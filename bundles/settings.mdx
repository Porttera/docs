---
title: "Settings"
description: "Configure bundle pricing and display."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pricing type" icon="dollar-sign">
    **Fixed** sets one price for the whole bundle. **Percentage off** discounts the combined
    total of the items.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Savings badge" icon="badge-percent">
    Show the amount or percentage saved on the product page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Inventory" icon="boxes-stacked">
    Bundles respect each item's stock, so you never oversell a component.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Placement" icon="object-group">
    Show the bundle on a product page, a dedicated page, or both.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Bundle items that genuinely go together — a clear, useful set converts better than a random
  discount.
</Tip>
